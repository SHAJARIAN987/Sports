Biography

Born Febuary 5th, 1985, Christiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveirois one of the world's most prominent soccer player. He was born and raised in Madeira. He began his senior club career for Sporting CP. Then, he signed with Manchester United in 2003. At Euro 2004, he was able to score his first international goal in his life. In 2008, he Ronaldo became the captain of Portugal's national team. During 2013, 2014, 2016, and 2017, he won Balloons d'Or. Ronaldo signed with Juventus in 2018. The initial transition was worth 100 million.
